Archaius tigris - Seychelles tiger chameleon

Archaius tigris CAS165616 Skull by Blackburn Lab on Sketchfab

This is an unlabeled 3D model. It is a really derived skull (lots of unique features). The crest and teeth are unique to this chameleon. Nonetheless, you can still find the features that you identified in Smaug. Try to find them on this chameleon. Note: most of the teeth of this chameleon are on the premaxilla, not the maxilla.
