Student Comments

Comments on drawing ability

  • I hope that the class is offered again so others can learn to draw the world around them just a little bit better and know that they can draw just fine.
  • It was a good confidence booster for people not so artistically inclined in the drawing department.
  • I liked looking at my sketchbook and seeing my improvement…I thought I could never learn to draw but I proved to myself that I could.
  • I felt that this drawing class really helped me to improve my drawing ability. (The instructor) helped us to realize the importance of truly examining the object that we are drawing, rather than using our imagination and draw what we thought we were seeing.
  • …I have learned to draw what I actually see instead of what I think I should see…
  • I did learn to pay attention to details. I became an all around better artist by merely using something that I had taken for granted, the ability to see and pay attention to detail.

On the relationship between Drawing and Biology

  • I didn’t think there was a relationship but after the class, I paid more attention to details.
  • I think it is great to know how to draw for biology. Becoming a better artist will help me to study better to fully understand the material.
  • I have realized, during my labs, how much more attention I pay to what I am trying to draw.
  • Before I took the drawing class, I would have drawn a worm like a long skinny line and not given it its true justice of what it is really composed of. Our (biology) tests asked us specifically for features of certain organisms. The drawings helped me remember what the organism that we were studying looked like. It helped me not only learn how to draw but also with my studying for biology…
  • In labs, we did a great amount of sketching and knowing how to observe the organism helped me draw a more accurate picture of the organisms we studied.
