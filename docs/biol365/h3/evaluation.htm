Pre and Post Instructional Drawings
In order to asses the development of skill the throughout the semester, students were asked to complete drawings of trees both on the first and last day of class. The first set of drawings showed a dependence on learned symbols of trees and looked "cartoonish". The second set of drawings more accurately represented the specific trees that were used as models.
